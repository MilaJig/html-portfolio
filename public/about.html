About Me

Mila once accidentally reverse-engineered the Matrix while trying to automate her morning coffee. A retired intergalactic 3D wizard, she now spends her time taming Blender scripts, interrogating polygons, and whispering sweet nothings to rogue Go routines. With a degree in Econometrics and a PhD in Guessing What Broke This Time™, Mila thrives on solving complex problems, especially the ones she caused herself. When she’s not debugging reality, she enjoys long walks through vertex forests and casual conversations with AI.
